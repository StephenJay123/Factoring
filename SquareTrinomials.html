How To Factor Perfect Square Trinomials

By Stephen Jay

Perfect Square Trinomials: A trinomial that can be expressed as a binomial squared

Equation:

a² + 2ab + b² = (a + b)²

OR

a² - 2ab + b² = (a - b)²

Example:

How To Solve:

The first step in factoring Perfect Square Trinomials is to solve for a and b
This is done by getting the square root of a as well as the square root of b

The second step in factoring Perfect Square Trinomials is to use the formula (a + b)²
Substitute your a and b values into the formula and that will be your answer.

Once you have substituted in these values, double check to ensure you cannot factor further.
After you've checked and factored if needed, the result is your final answer.
