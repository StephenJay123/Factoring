How To Factor Difference of Squares

By Stephen Jay

Difference Of Squares: a Binomial with two perfect squares, a positive first term and negative second term.

Equation:

a² - b² = (a + b)(a - b)

How To Solve:

The first step in factoring with Difference of Squares is to put the square root of a at the front of the two brackets
Follow this up by getting the square root of b and adding it to a in the first bracket and subtracing it from a in the second.

In the example above, that is the final answer.
